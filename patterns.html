Cute and modern blackwork embroidery patterns to download

Blackwork embroidery is a fun and relaxing needlework technique that is very similar to cross stitch. Stitches are made using backstitch and can be faster to stitch than cross stitch pieces. Blackwork is easy to pick up and all my patterns include a quick 'How to guide'. You can find out all about it in my article - what is blackwork embroidery?
