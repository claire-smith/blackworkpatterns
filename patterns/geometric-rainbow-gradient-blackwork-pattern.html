  • Fabric requiredWhite 14 count Aida or 28 count evenweave

Modern blackwork pattern of a repeating rainbow, geometric design. Designed to fit in an 8 inch hoop. Small repeating pattern in 10 colors of the rainbow. Makes for a lovely home wall piece of art.

Pattern is designed to fit an oval 8 inch hoop frame with 14 count Aida but can easily be adapted to fit a variety of different shape and sized frames due to the nature of the repeating design.

All stitches are full backstitch.

Once stitched it is easy to frame in a wooden hoop / flexi hoop (available at most craft stores) and looks great as wall art in your home.
